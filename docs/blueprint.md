# **App Name**: DMED Insights Board

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Content Layout: Render all informational blocks (header, key metrics, benefits, AI sections) and their respective data, text, and logos on a single, continuously horizontal canvas.
- Interactive Metric Displays: Showcase key performance indicators (KPIs) in visually distinct, highlightable card modules with large numbers and modern medical-digital icons.
- Dual Benefit Showcase: Display structured content in two columns for 'Patients' and 'Doctors', each with dedicated digital medical icons and clear bullet points detailing benefits.
- Advanced AI Feature Presentation: Dedicated visual sections to explain DMED's AI implementations, including specific solution details (AI TOP3-DSS, AI STROKE) and illustrative large data cards for general AI statistics.
- AI-Powered Explainer Tool: Utilize generative AI to provide on-demand, context-aware summaries or expanded explanations for selected DMED features or statistics, tailoring the output for different simulated audience profiles upon user request within the display interface.
- Official Branding Integration: Precisely incorporate and position the logos of the Ministry of Health, UZINFOCOM, and DMED in the header section, ensuring official and solemn presentation.
- High-Tech Visual Effects: Apply subtle soft glow effects, technological lines, thin contours, and translucent glass-style cards to achieve a premium, modern, and engaging aesthetic as specified by the user.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background: A deep, authoritative digital blue (#0F0F3D) forms the foundation, establishing a technologically advanced and official atmosphere for the information board.
- Primary (Key Headers): A rich, contrasting golden-orange (#CC831A) is designated for crucial titles, providing a warm focal point that conveys importance and premium quality against the dark background.
- Accent: A vibrant, clear sky-blue (#61CBED) serves for subtle neon highlights, interactive elements, and modern visual details, strategically chosen to complement the deep blue background and provide distinct contrast with the golden-orange primary.
- Body and Headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, objective clarity and exceptional legibility across all information blocks, aligning perfectly with a professional, strict, and technological presentation style.
- A consistent set of clean, minimalist line icons, rendered in a medical-digital aesthetic with soft glowing outlines, will be used throughout for data points, benefits, and conceptual elements to reinforce the high-tech theme.
- The layout strictly adheres to a single-page horizontal format, optimized for widescreen displays (16:9 or A1 landscape), ensuring a fluid left-to-right and top-to-bottom information flow with ample visual 'air' between distinct logical blocks.
- Subtle, smooth hover and glow animations will be applied to data cards, icons, and interactive components. These discreet animations enhance interactivity and underscore the premium, futuristic feel without introducing distractions.
